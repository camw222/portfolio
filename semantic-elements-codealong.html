Sharks

Sharks are the coolest animals.
They are the apex predators of the oceans, and there are hundreds of different types. Their variation makes them the most interesting kind of animal.

Bats

Bats are also very interesting animals. Their echolocation is a fascinating hunting technique and they are also so cute.

Conclusion

To summarize, bats are cool but not as cool as sharks.

But they are still awesome animals.
